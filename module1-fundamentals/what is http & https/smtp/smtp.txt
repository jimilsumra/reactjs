What is SMTP?

Def: The full form of SMTP is the Simple Mail Transfer Protocol. SMTP is a communication protocol to send emails on the network and ensuring
the email is transferred from the sender's email client to the recipient's mail server. 

SMTP is mainly used by many well-known email providers like Gmail, Yahoo Mail, Outlook etc. Other than some of these well-known email
providers, other hosting providers also provide the mail server. In this case, SMTP uses the POP3 and IMAP.
