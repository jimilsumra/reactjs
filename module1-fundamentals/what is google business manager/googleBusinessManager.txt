What is Google Business Manager?

Def: Google Business Manager is a free service provided by Google to allow businesses to create & manage their business presence on Google
search and maps. 

Businesses can manage all the below information for their business:
- business address,
- phone numbers & email addresses for contact.
- website link, 
- photos 
- customer reviews 
- promote offers

Using the Google Business Manager, makes it easier for potential customers to find your business contact details, reviews, photos & website links.
